112 Process

112 is the Integrated emergency management system of Uttar Pradesh Police, which provides prompt help to promote help to person(s) in need at anytime,anywhere in Uttar Pradesh.It stans strong on the pillars of advanced technology, better process and coordinated action.

How 112 Works-


1.The communication officer register the distress information given by the caller.
2.The dispatch officer transmits the regsitered details to the Police Response Vehicle(PRV),available nearest to the caller.
3.Police Response Vehicle(PRV)reaches the caller's location or where the help is required, in the minimum possible time.
4.Obtaining feedback from the informer.



The salient Features of 112-

1.112 is a 24x7 emergency service.Any call on 112 from all 75 districts of Uttar Pradesh lands direvtly at 112 conatct center.
Anybody can register an emergency by dialing 112.The call is received by women Communication Officers who are non-police offiers,trained and empathetic.The entire conversation between caller and Communication Officer is recorded.At the request of the caller,his/her anoynymity can be maintained.
2.After registration of the information,it is digitally transmitted to the Dispatch Officer who then dispatches it to the nearest Police REsponse Vehicle (PRV)on their Mobile Data Termina(MDT).Also, the information is sent to the concerned Police Station in-charge and senior Offficers on their mobile phones.
