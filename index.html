112 Process

Anyone may reach 112 through Phone,SMS or Social Media. All calls go through PRI lines and are transferred to Avaya. Then later transferred to CO. CO greets according to the language,dialect and speaks empathetically.

  • Voice gets recorded and stored.
  • CO Classifies calls as Actionable.
  • DO Validates,assesses and prioritizes the call and takes action as per SOP.
  • SDO receives the call if required and take action as per SOP.
  • SDO closes high priority calls.

A SMART Police Organisation

In November 2014 Prime Minister of India Shri Narendra Modi exhorted all police organization to become "SMART".He emphasized that Policeshould be Strict and Sensitive, Modern and Mobile, Alert and Accountable, Reliable and Responsive and Trained and Techno-savvy.

  • 'Savera'- a ray of hope in the lives of senior citizens
  • The pre-registered details like address, location help in giving 'enhanced response' when 112 is called
  • The senior citizen may get the primary registration done by calling 112.
  • After primary registration, the local Police Station or Beat Officer goes to the residence of the senior citizen and does the detailed registration, in which essential details are sought.​
